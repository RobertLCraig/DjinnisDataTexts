# Artwork & Logo Prompt Suggestions

Prompt ideas for generating logos, icons, and banner art for Djinni's Data Texts.
Use with image generation tools (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, etc.).

---

## Logo / Icon

### Minimal Glyph
> A clean, flat icon of a stylized djinni lamp or genie bottle with a small data readout or HUD element floating above it. Dark background, gold and teal accent colors. Suitable for a 64x64 game addon icon. Digital art, sharp edges, no text.

### Fantasy-Tech Fusion
> A mystical djinni lantern fused with a holographic data display. Glowing teal numbers and stats float in wisps of golden smoke. Square icon format, dark slate background. World of Warcraft art style, crisp lines, suitable as a software icon.

### Arcane Data Crystal
> A floating arcane crystal orb with miniature data readouts (FPS, coordinates, gold coins) visible inside. Purple and gold color scheme. Fantasy UI element style. Clean, iconic, 1:1 aspect ratio.

### Scroll & Runes
> An ancient scroll partially unrolled, with glowing runic text that resembles data readouts (numbers, bars, percentages). Gold illumination on dark parchment. Warcraft-inspired fantasy calligraphy style.

## Banner / Header Art (for CurseForge, Wago, GitHub)

### Panoramic Dashboard
> A wide panoramic banner showing a fantasy adventurer's desk with a magical holographic heads-up display floating above it. The HUD shows gold counts, dungeon lockout timers, map coordinates, and performance stats rendered in arcane script. Warm candlelight mixed with teal magical glow. Warcraft concept art style. 16:5 aspect ratio.

### Tooltip Showcase
> A dark-themed banner with multiple floating tooltip panels arranged in a fan layout, each showing different data: guild roster, currency totals, FPS/latency, coordinates. Panels have dark backgrounds with gold headers and subtle glow edges. Clean UI mockup aesthetic with a fantasy twist. 16:5 aspect ratio.

### Djinni's Workshop
> A mystical djinni's workshop filled with floating data orbs, each displaying different game information (clock, map, gold pile, crossed swords). A djinni figure made of golden smoke tends the orbs. Rich, warm fantasy palette. Digital painting, wide banner format.

## CurseForge Thumbnail (16:9)

### Data Stream
> A stream of golden magical data flowing from a djinni's lamp, forming readable stats and numbers as it arcs through a dark void. Teal and gold color scheme. Eye-catching, suitable as a project thumbnail. 16:9 aspect ratio.

### Module Grid
> A 3x3 grid of small glowing icons representing addon modules: a sword (character), coins (currency), clock (time), map pin (coordinates), envelope (mail), gauge (performance), shield (instances), paw print (pets), speedometer (movement). Each icon in a rounded dark tile with a subtle glow. Flat design, Warcraft-inspired colors.

## Style Keywords to Include

Append these to any prompt for consistent results:

- **Art style**: `World of Warcraft concept art`, `Blizzard art style`, `fantasy UI design`, `digital painting`
- **Color palette**: `dark slate background`, `gold (#FFD100) accents`, `teal (#66C7FF) highlights`, `warm amber glow`
- **Mood**: `mystical`, `arcane`, `information-rich`, `organized`, `elegant`
- **Technical**: `sharp edges`, `clean lines`, `high contrast`, `suitable for dark backgrounds`, `no text unless specified`

## WoW Addon Icon (Interface\Icons format)

For the in-game TOC `IconTexture`, the icon should work at 64x64 and 32x32:

> A djinni's lamp viewed from the front, simplified to work at small sizes. Golden lamp body with a wisp of teal smoke forming a tiny bar chart or data readout. Dark background. Flat shading, bold outlines, pixel-clear at 32px. No text.

> Alternatively: A golden letter "D" with arcane circuit-like patterns running through it, teal glow on a dark background. Monogram style, legible at 32x32.
